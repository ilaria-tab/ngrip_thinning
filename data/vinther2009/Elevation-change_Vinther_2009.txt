All ages are given in years b2k (years relative to A.D. 2000). 
Age values refer to the bottom of each interval. 
Thus the age value 60 b2k refer to the interval 40 - 60 b2k. 
Site elevation data has been corrected for upstream effects.  
These are the data shown in fig. 2a. 
The estimated 1-sigma uncertainties on both site elevation data 
are +/- 40 m for GRIP and NGRIP, and +/- 65 m for Camp Century 
and DYE-3 (see Vinther et al., 2009 suppl. inf. for details).
